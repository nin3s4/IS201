Tableau Dashboards


One amazing concept that I have recently learned about are dashboards.
They are a tool for people in management positions in businesses to be
able to take a quick glance at how their company is doing. Lets take a
look into a superstore based in the USA.

How are profits doing in the 50 states of the United States? No problem! Take a quick glace to see what states are doing better than others. Texas is not going very good.

Want to inspect one state at the time? Done. Above is a chart of how California's profit is doing. As you can see from the chart to the left, California is one of the top with the highest profit.

Lets take a closer look into California. We can inspect how profits are for each prodcut category and discuss how we can use this information to figure out how much focus we should give each product.

We can even create a quick forecast to prepare for the future! Looking at the graph above, next November we'll hit record breaking sales!




The most amazing part of it all is that they update the moment there is
a change! As new information comes in, people in management will have peace
of mind knowing that they are gettinng the most recent, updated information
about their company!
